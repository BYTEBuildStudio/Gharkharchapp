Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

Welcome to GharKharch. Your privacy is paramount. GharKharch works fundamentally as an offline-first local-first application.

1. Data Collection and Usage

All household expenses, budgets, category profiles, and head metadata are stored completely inside your own local browser storage (Room Web emulation). No data leaves your machine or is uploaded to our servers.

2. Google Sheets Authentication Sync

When you sync your transactions, they compile directly in your browser and append onto your specified Google Drive spreadsheet securely using direct HTTPS Google APIs keys. GharKharch never gathers or saves your Google credentials, passwords, or data logs.

3. Third-party Links

We do not use metrics or analytics trackers on our platforms, maintaining a pure sandbox interface.
